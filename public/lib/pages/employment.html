Employment

Tangentyere Council is the peak organization of the Alice Springs Town Camp residents. It also provides a range of services to the wider Alice Springs community and to remote Indigenous communities. Today Tangentyere provides housing, maintenance, employment and training, law and justice, family, youth and aged care, financial and banking, advocacy services to Indigenous people across the Central Australian Region.

How To Apply

If you wish to apply for a position at Tangentyere Council you must do all of the following:

  • Complete an application which addresses the Selection Criteria for the position;
  • Nominate three referees, of whom at least two are aware of your current or recent work history;
  • Attach your CV (curriculum vitae);
  • Mail your completed application with CV attached to

    HR Manager (Confidential)
    Tangentyere Council
    PO Box 8070
    Alice Springs, NT 0871
